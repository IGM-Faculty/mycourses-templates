Important Dates

  • 8/30: Last day of Add/Drop period

The times at which assignments are due on the dates shown depend on their type:

  • Practice exercises must be demonstrated to an instructor or TA by the end of class on the due date.
  • Exams must be completed within a given 24 hour period and you'll have only a set amount of time to finish based on when you start.
  • All other assignments (quizzes, homeworks, project deliverables, etc.) are always due by 11:59pm ET on the due date.
