Passing the Class (i.e. the "GDAPS 70% threshold rule")

A grade of 70% (C-) or higher is required to pass this course and move on to IGME-106. This threshold applies to the overall course grade AND the homework and exam elements!

  • AVERAGE OVERALL GRADE OF 70% REQUIRED TO PASS
  • AVERAGE HOMEWORK GRADE OF 70% REQUIRED TO PASS
  • AVERAGE WEIGHTED EXAM GRADE OF 70% REQUIRED TO PASS

In other words, if your individual exam or homework average is less than 70%, you will not earn a passing grade regardless of your performance in other areas of the course.

This rule is department policy, applies to all sections of this course, and will not be waived for any reason.

If at any time, you are concerned about your performance or progress in this course, you should not hesitate to contact your instructor and discuss it.
